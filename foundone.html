⚡ Girth 1. The circumference of any circular object. 2. The distance around a column.
⚡ Debris 1. pieces of wood, metal, building materials, etc. that are left after something has been destroyed.
⚡ Excavate 1. to make a hole in the ground by digging, 2. removal of Earth for construction purposes.
⚡ NGL 1. Natural Ground Level 2. level of the site in its unmodified state, or 3. in a state which has been graded.
⚡ Extra over 1. can be used to refer to items that to a certain extent have already been measured, but have not been priced at the full value of all their labour and materials.
⚡ Soft rock 1. sedimentary rock, 2. Typically medium to fine-grained, easily eroded and comparably easier to excavate in.
⚡ Hard rock 1. Igneous and metamorphic rock, 2. typically large grained, resistant to weathering and caparably harder to excavate in.
⚡ Mod AASHTO 1. Modified American Association of State Highway and Transportation Officials, 2. A measure of the required dry density and moisture content at which compaction should take place.
⚡ Backfilling 1. the process of putting soil back into an excavation once related works has been completed.
