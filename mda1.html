DEF. Girth 1. The circumference of any circular object. 2. The distance around a column.
DEF. Debris 1. pieces of wood, metal, building materials, etc. that are left after something has been destroyed.
DEF. Excavate 1. to make a hole in the ground by digging, 2. removal of Earth for construction purposes.
DEF. NGL 1. Natural Ground Level 2. level of the site in its unmodified state, or 3. in a state which has been graded.
DEF. Extra over 1. can be used to refer to items that to a certain extent have already been measured, but have not been priced at the full value of all their labour and materials.
DEF. Soft rock 1. sedimentary rock, 2. Typically medium to fine-grained, easily eroded and comparably easier to excavate in.
DEF. Hard rock 1. Igneous and metamorphic rock, 2. typically large grained, resistant to weathering and caparably harder to excavate in.
DEF. Mod AASHTO 1. Modified American Association of State Highway and Transportation Officials, 2. A measure of the required dry density and moisture content at which compaction should take place.
DEF. Backfilling 1. the process of putting soil back into an excavation once related works has been completed.
